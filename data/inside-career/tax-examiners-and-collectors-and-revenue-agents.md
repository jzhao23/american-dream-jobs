People who thrive in tax enforcement combine tax knowledge with investigative instinct and the backbone to enforce laws against taxpayers who may be hostile or evasive. Successful practitioners develop expertise in tax law while building the interpersonal skills to handle confrontational situations professionally. They must balance enforcement rigor with fairness and maintain objectivity despite taxpayer pressure.
